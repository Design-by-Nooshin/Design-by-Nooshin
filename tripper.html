Introduction

  • In this project, I designed an app for a travel planner application for planning journeys with the Randstad Rail.
  • Randstad Rail were rebranding themselves as Tripp3r and asked to redesign their new mobile application.
  • This was one of the projects I did during my studies at The Hague University of applied sciences.

  • At first I tried to sketch all of the "must have"s and then proceeding to "should have"s and "could have"s.

Low-Fi prototype

  • In this stage I did not pay any attention to how my app looks like in terms of icons and colors and I kept it as simple as possible.
  • I did the design in Adobe XD. You can see the Low-Fi prototype below:

Low-Fi - test

  • I used a qualitative testing protocol in which I ask the user to perform some tasks and then I observe their behavior while doing the tasks.
  • I kept an eye on what they say, the way they act, whether they are confused or not.
  • Also, I recorded their interaction with the wireframe so that later I can extract points insights it.

Hi-Fi prototype
